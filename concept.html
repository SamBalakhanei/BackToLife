The Concepts

A neural network is defined as a computer system modeled on the human brain. Just as the human brain is able to recognize patterns, computers can do the same. Through supervised learning, a computer algorithm is trained based on the data from various inputs and outputs. By detecting the patterns between the two, the computer can eventually predict the output with an input that it has never seen before. For this algorithm, millions of images were processed and analyzed by the computer, thus resulting in super accurate colorization.
