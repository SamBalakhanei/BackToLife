How does it work?


A neural network is first trained to recognize patterns that indicate how a photograph is colored. This system of supervised learning is combined with Python scripts in order to colorize these grayscale images.



Can I colorize?

Send your own photo to OfficialBTLSB@gmail.com and you'll receieve the best attempt at colorization in your email ASAP.
